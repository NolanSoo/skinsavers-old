Welcome to Skinsavers! This app allows you to quickly self-diagnose yourself for skin cancer, potentially saving you thousands of dollars or even years of your life. All you need to do is input at least one image of your skin (multiple is better). Afterward, you will know if you have skin cancer or not. If you do, you will quickly receive priceless information such as its type, its progression through different parts of the body, and next steps, such as effective and easy treatment options.
